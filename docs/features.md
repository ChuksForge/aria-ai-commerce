# Features & Integration

## Overview

ARIA is designed as an intelligence layer for modern e-commerce platforms.

It enhances product discovery, improves customer decision-making, and increases revenue per session without requiring a full platform rebuild.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 💬 Conversational Stylist

Transforms natural language into structured shopping intent.

* Understands occasion, budget, and style preferences
* Maintains context within a session
* Guides users toward relevant products through dialogue

**Impact:** reduces friction in product discovery and shortens time to purchase

---

### 🔍 Semantic Search

Moves beyond keyword matching to intent-based retrieval.

* Matches meaning instead of exact terms
* Refines results based on context and relevance
* Produces explainable recommendations

**Impact:** reduces empty searches and improves result quality

---

### 👗 Outfit Generation

Builds complete looks from a single product.

* Generates coordinated multi-item recommendations
* Ensures consistency in style and context
* Uses only available catalogue items

**Impact:** increases average order value and cross-sell performance

---

### ⭐ Review Intelligence

Converts unstructured reviews into decision-ready insights.

* Extracts pros, cons, and sizing signals
* Summarises sentiment across multiple dimensions
* Highlights key customer feedback

**Impact:** increases purchase confidence and reduces returns

---

## Integration Options

### 1. Embedded Assistant

ARIA is deployed as a branded assistant within your storefront.

* Customised to match your brand
* Connected to your catalogue
* Hosted and maintained

**Best for:** fast deployment with minimal internal resources

---

### 2. API Integration

ARIA acts as a backend intelligence layer.

* Your frontend, ARIA’s intelligence
* Structured responses for full UI control
* Flexible integration into existing systems

**Best for:** teams with in-house engineering capability

---

### 3. Lightweight Widget

Plug-and-play integration via a single script.

* Minimal setup
* Works with most storefront platforms
* Rapid experimentation

**Best for:** quick validation and smaller teams

---

## Integration Requirements

To deploy ARIA, the following inputs are needed:

* Product catalogue (API, JSON, or CSV)
* Brand configuration (visual and tone)
* Review data (optional but recommended)

---

## Commercial Model

ARIA is offered as a flexible solution depending on integration level:

* **Initial setup** — configuration and integration
* **Ongoing usage** — based on traffic or API volume
* **Optional support** — maintenance and optimisation

Custom pricing is determined based on catalogue size and expected usage.

ARIA integrates into existing e-commerce systems to provide:

* better product discovery
* higher conversion rates
* increased basket size

without requiring a platform rebuild.
